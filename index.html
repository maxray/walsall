Abundance in Walsall

A project to profile the wealth of arts and cultural activities available in Walsall town, through the creation of an interactive, digital arts map & directory.

With your support we hope to commission an artist to create a series of illustrations detailing Walsallâ€™s:

  • Heritage Buildings & Sites
  • Arts Venues
  • Creative Studios
  • Leisure & Activity Spaces

These illustrations will be curated into the online illustrated map to provide a new way for the public to engage with Walsall's heritage, culture & creative scene, whilst creating a useful directory for creative organisations to profile their ventures, venues & activities.

We will be working with an artist based in Birmingham & the Black Country - Tom Woolley . He is a prominent illustrator within the west midlands, who specialises in illustrated maps.

To support in developing the map we will work in partnership with Walsall New Art Gallery, Walsall Council (Walsall for All), local businesses, artists and residents to identify the most important items to illustrate.

Our project aims to:

  • Reduce the talent loss from the town to neighbouring towns with more prominent creative infrastructure; Wolverhampton and Birmingham.
  • Help increase visibility and use of venues, activities and creative practices within the town.
  • Profile cultural assets in order to help develop a shared sense of pride in Walsall
  • Aid to support the actioning of experiments and development of new creative ventures in the locality.
